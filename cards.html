Sensors vs Actuators

Sensors: Devices that detect physical changes (such as temperature, pressure, or motion) and convert them into electrical signals for measurement or monitoring.

Actuators: Devices that convert electrical signals into physical actions, such as movement, heat, or control over a mechanism.
